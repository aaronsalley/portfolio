## üìò 4. Hiring Smarter & Coaching Better

**Hiring with specificity:**

- Define whether the role is Core, Growth, etc.
- From there, use the skill matrix: e.g., a senior Growth PM must master experimentation, funnels, stakeholder influence, and roadmap setting.

**Interview structure:**

- **Specialist round**: focus deeply on specialization (e.g., platform architecture or growth metrics).
- **Generalist round**: test across other product skills‚Äîcustomer empathy, team leadership, communication.
- **Leadership round**: assess mentoring ability, product strategy, vision articulation.

**Coaching approach:**

- For specialists: prioritize domain skills early; later, broaden into discovery, strategy, executive communication.

## üöÄ 5. Roadmap for PMs: Grow from APM to CPO

### Early career (APM ‚Üí PM):

- Build a **T-shaped profile**: strong in general product fundamentals, with budding specialization ([Medium][1]).
- Choose **one specialization** and ramp quickly on deep skills.

### Mid-career (PM ‚Üí Lead PM):

- Take ownership of domain-specific outcomes (e.g., key growth metrics or new platform features).
- Begin mentoring junior PMs using the matrix as a development guide.

### Senior (Lead ‚Üí Executive):

- Transition to breadth: lead across product domains.
- Shape strategy, culture, and product vision.
- Use matrix for org-wide hiring and team enablement.
